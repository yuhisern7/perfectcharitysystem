Internal operations:

The donation money is not used to pay for the expenses in PCS internal operations, the donations are separated, it mostly consist of volunteers. For workers such as IT staffs their full details and salaries are visible to the public in their PCS profiles through our blockchain system.

External operations:

These are for those that seek to physically help the suffering poor, volunteer or seek to get paid via projects, their contributions will be recorded in their profiles.
